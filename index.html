My introduction

I am a System Analysis and Development student, aspiring to become a native mobile app developer.

The reasoning being that we spend a lot of time on our smartphones and we will become more and more dependent on them for our daily routines, so it makes sense to learn how to build apps for our phones which is what I'm currently seeking.
